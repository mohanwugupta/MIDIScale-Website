Our Team

The experts behind MIDIScale

Mohan Gupta

CEO & Founder

Mohan is a leading researcher and PhD candidate in motor sequence learning (piano learning) at UC San Diego. He tried learning the piano through piano learning software but realized it lacked a personalized experience and the software had no idea what he was learning. Thus, with his expertise as a premier learning scientist, he set out to make piano learning software that actually measures learning and uses that to help the user.

Vijay Adusumalli
